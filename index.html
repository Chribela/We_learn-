Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the right courses for me?

Finding the right courses for yourself involves a thoughtful and introspective process. Here are some steps to help you identify the courses that align with your interests, goals, and strengths

What is online learning, and how does it work?

Online learning, also known as e-learning, is a method of education that takes place entirely over the internet. Students can access their lessons, assignments, and interact with teachers through our virtual learning platform

What technology requirements are needed ?

To participate in online learning, students will need a computer or laptop with internet access. Additionally, having a webcam and a headset with a microphone is beneficial for virtual discussions and live sessions.

How can I enroll my child for online classes?

To enroll your child in our online learning program, please visit our website's Admissions page and follow the registration process outlined there.

Is it effective as traditional classroom learning?

Yes, online learning can be just as effective as traditional learning. Our teachers are dedicated to providing engaging and interactive online lessons that promote active learning and encourage student participation.

Are there schedules for online learning?

Yes, we follow a structured class schedule for online learning. Students will have specific timings for live classes, and course materials will be available for self-paced learning as well.

What tools and platforms do you use for teaching?

We use a combination of online tools and platforms for teaching, which may include virtual classrooms, learning management systems, video conferencing software, and interactive educational apps.

Can students chat with their teachers

Absolutely! Our online learning environment encourages active participation and collaboration. Students can interact with teachers and classmates through discussion forums, video conferencing, and virtual group projects.

What resources are available ?

We provide a variety of resources to support online learning, including digital textbooks, educational videos, supplemental materials, and access to an extensive online library.

How are assessments conducted in online learning?

Assessments and exams are conducted securely through our online learning platform. Depending on the course, students may have quizzes, assignments, and occasionally, proctored online exams.

Are the courses beginner friendly🤔
